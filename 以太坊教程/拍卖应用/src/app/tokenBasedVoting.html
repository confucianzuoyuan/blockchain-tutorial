Decentralized Voting Application (Ropsten Testnet)

How to use the app

Step 1: Install the metamask plugin and create an account on Ropsten Test Network and load some Ether. DM @zastrinlab if you need test ether.

Step 2: Purchase tokens below by entering the total number of tokens you like to buy.

Step 3: Vote for candidates by entering their name and no. of tokens to vote with.

Step 4: Enter your account address to look up your voting activity.
